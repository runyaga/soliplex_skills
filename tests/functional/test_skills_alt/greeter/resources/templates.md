# Greeting Templates

## Formal

- "Good morning, {name}. How may I assist you today?"
- "Welcome, {name}. It's a pleasure to meet you."

## Casual

- "Hey {name}! What's up?"
- "Hi {name}, nice to see you!"

## Professional

- "Hello {name}, thank you for reaching out."
- "Greetings {name}, I'm here to help."
