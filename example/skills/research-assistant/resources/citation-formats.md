# Citation Format Templates

## APA (7th Edition)

### Journal Article
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical*,
volume(issue), page-page. https://doi.org/xxxxx

### Book
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work: Capital letter for subtitle* (Edition).
Publisher. https://doi.org/xxxxx

### Website
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). *Title of page*. Site Name. URL

## MLA (9th Edition)

### Journal Article
Author(s). "Title of Article." *Title of Journal*, vol. #, no. #, Year,
pp. #-#. Database, doi.

### Book
Author(s). *Title of Book*. Publisher, Year.

## IEEE

### Journal Article
[#] A. Author and B. Author, "Title of article," *Abbrev. Title of Journal*,
vol. #, no. #, pp. #-#, Month Year, doi: xxxxx.

### Conference Paper
[#] A. Author, "Title of paper," in *Proc. Conf. Name*, City, Country, Year,
pp. #-#.

## Chicago (17th Edition)

### Journal Article
Author Last, First. "Article Title." *Journal Title* Volume, no. Issue (Year):
Page-Page. https://doi.org/xxxxx.

### Book
Author Last, First. *Book Title*. Place: Publisher, Year.
